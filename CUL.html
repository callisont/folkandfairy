To find copies of these items to read, I suggest your local library. If they don't own the item themselves, ask them to InterLibrary Loan it for you (you can also check to see if libraries near you own it by searching for the item in http://www.worldcat.org )

To purchase, I recommend using an independent used-book seller such as ABE books
